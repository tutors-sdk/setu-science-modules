## Module Aim

This module includes the identification, classification, nomenclature, ecology and geographic range of the major tree species of Ireland, and the more common understory vegetation associated with forestry. Students will learn to use dichotomous keys in the field identification of trees. This module will also provide a comprehensive knowledge of the silviculture and uses of the main Irish forest tree species.

---

## Learning Outcomes

On successful completion of this module, learners will be able to:

1. Identify the major tree species of Ireland from living specimens as well as from samples of flowers, twigs, leaves, fruits, and written descriptions.
2. Use rules of scientific nomenclature to correctly present the common and binomial names.
3. Employ dichotomous plant keys to identify unknown species.
4. State major and minor uses of each species.
5. Describe physical, biological, ecological and silvicultural features associated with the major tree/forest types in Ireland.

---

## Indicative Content

The module covers the following topics:

- Introduction to dendrology
- Rules and use of scientific classification and nomenclature
- Building and use of dichotomous plant keys
- Conifers and broadleaves (three field trips)
