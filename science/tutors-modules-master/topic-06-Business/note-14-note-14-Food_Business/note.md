## Module Aim

This course aims to introduce the student to the strategy of food business in Ireland, the EU and internationally. A broad review of the environment in which it operates culminating in a student led quantitative and qualitative research into new consumer products in Ireland.

---

## Learning Outcomes

On successful completion of this module, learners will be able to:

1. Analyse the strategies of key players operating in the food business, both locally and globally.
2. Examine and identify the market forces influencing the development of food business strategy.
3. Complete both qualitative and quantitative food business research.
4. Develop a food business report and recommend appropriate strategies.
5. Prepare presentation and defend report findings.

---

## Indicative Content

The module covers the following topics:

- Food business strategy in Ireland, the EU and internationally
- The market forces influencing the main players in both local and global markets
- Research of food market trends and selected food companies' strategies
- Application of both qualitative and quantitative consumer food research methods
- Research, compilation and presentation of a food business report
