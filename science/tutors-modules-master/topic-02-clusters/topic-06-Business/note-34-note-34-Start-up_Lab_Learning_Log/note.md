## Module Aim

The Start-up Lab learning log is an important tool in the development of reflective learning skills. The log should provide a reflection on key learning opportunities the student was exposed to during the semester. These can include academic, work based or personal development learning opportunities. Students should integrate their learning on a practical and academic level i.e. bring theoretical learning and knowledge, past and present, to bear on practical 'learning experiences' through reflection and vice versa.

---

## Learning Outcomes

On successful completion of this module, learners will be able to:

1. Complete a reflective learning log for the flexible semester.
2. Appraise own capabilities, competences and skills to start-up and run an enterprise.
3. Communicate how they have applied academic and practical knowledge and skills to their own start-up idea.
4. Communicate practical learning experiences from undertaking the Start-up Lab module.
5. Generate key personal and professional outcomes from undertaking the Start-up Lab module.

---

## Indicative Content

The module covers the following topics:

- Overview of the nature and purpose of a reflective learing log
- Overview of key techniques in developing a reflective learning log
- Choice and description of learning activities
- Abstraction of learning to theory and personal development or transferable skills
- Learning logs as a means of personal evaluation and action

## Assessment Criteria

Marks and the weighting of each topic to be addressed on the template will be allocated for the learning log as follows:

Choice and description of learning activities 15%

Critical reflection on skills, competences and capabilities developed 55%

Key outcomes (academic and personal development ) 10%

Personal evaluation and action 20%
