## Module Aim

To provide students with knowledge and practical skills to use financial information to manage working capital, evaluate an investment opportunity in financial terms, source finance and comply with obligations relating to Value Added Tax (VAT) in a small/medium enterprise (SME).

---

## Learning Outcomes

On successful completion of this module, learners will be able to:

1. Manage the elements of working capital in an SME.
2. Determine an appropriate investment opportunity in financial terms.
3. Determine the factors affecting sources of finance and describe appropriate finance options in an SME.
4. Complete the basic requirements of the VAT system.

---

## Indicative Content

The module covers the following topics:

- Introduction to finance: the finance function, role of financial management, insurable and uninsurable risks
- Working capital: management of the elements of working capital (inventory, receivables, payables, cash)
- Investment appraisal: discounting, Net Present Value (NPV), payback
- Financing: factors affecting the availability of finance, main sources of finance available to an SME, gearing
- VAT: how the VAT system operates, registration, records, returns
