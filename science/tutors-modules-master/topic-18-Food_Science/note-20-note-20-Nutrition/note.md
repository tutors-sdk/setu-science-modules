## Module Aim

This module introduces the study of nutrition and the foundations of healthy eating. It will cover nutrients in food, and their role in health and the application of nutritional knowledge.

---

## Learning Outcomes

On successful completion of this module, learners will be able to:

1. Define nutrition and discuss the basics of a healthy diet.
2. Identify ways of assessing nutritional status.
3. Discuss the influences on eating habits.
4. Discuss nutrients in various food groups.
5. Explain the role of nutrients in health.
6. Review diets using dietary analysis programmes.
7. Discuss the application of nutritional knowledge across the life span.

---

## Indicative Content

The module covers the following topics:

- Study of nutrition and food habits
- Assessing nutritional status
- Basics of a healthy diet
- Dietary analysis, assessing dietary intake
- The nutrients in food and their role in health: proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, minerals
- The application of nutritional knowledge, pregnancy and lactation, infants, children and adolescents, adults and the elderly population
