# Project 1 (Research)

This is the capstone module for Semester one; it should bring together the student?s knowledge on all aspects of IT and utilise many facets of their previous learning, with a view to identifying a suitable research problem that is to be investigated.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Professional Skills

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Appraise a research area.
- Create appropriate research questions.
- Complete a literature review.
- Select an appropriate research methodology and data gathering technique.

---

## Indicative Content

- Write succinct and referenced, well-structured reports
- Introduction
- Analysis of research questions
- Literature review
- Research methodology
