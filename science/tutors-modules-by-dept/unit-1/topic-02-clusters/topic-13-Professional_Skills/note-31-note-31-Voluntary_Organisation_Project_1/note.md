# Voluntary Organisation Proj 1

This module aims to allow students to experience a particular area of volunteering in Irish community.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 10
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Professional Skills

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Show enhanced communication & interpersonal skills.
- Employ personal autonomy and take responsibility for own work and behaviour in a work place.
- Demonstrate a greater level of management, analytical and teamwork skills.
- Integrate relevant practical work experience with academic theory.

---

## Indicative Content

- Students will be required to attend at their voluntary work-placement at the required dates and times and carry out their assigned duties in a professional manner
- Students will be required to meet with their voluntary work-place and college mentor to discuss their progress
- Students will be required to complete their work-placement as per their learning agreement
