# ICT Skills Placement

Students will spend a period of not less than four months in a work experience position in an ICT and Software Development workplace.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 30
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Professional Skills

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Apply knowledge, skills and competencies acquired on the academic to the analysis and creation of solutions to workplace problems.
- Contextualise the knowledge gained in the programme in an area relevant a selected area of interest.
- Communicate effectively in an appropriate and professional manner and format.
- Work as a member of a team and have developed appropriate communication and interpersonal skills.
- Reflect on and analyse the learning experience resulting from the work placement.
- Communicate the aims, expectations and objectives of all parties to the placement.

---

## Indicative Content

- Completion of agreed supervised project.
