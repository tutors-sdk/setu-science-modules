# Physics 1

This module is designed to introduce the computing student to the mechanics, electricity, light and measurement principles that they are likely to encounter in their computing course.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Introductory
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Mathematics and Physics

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Use the SI system of units to carry out formula-based calculations.
- Define the significant terms associated with mechanics, electricity, light and measurement.
- Describe and discuss the application of the physics concepts introduced related to a limited number of real world scenarios/devices.
- Use laboratory equipment in a safe and accurate manner.
- Report on and critically assess a range of experiments relating to the course content.

---

## Indicative Content

- Mechanics: SI units; vectors; Newtons laws; gravitation; projectiles; energy; conservation laws; uniform circular motion
- Electricity: Ohm's law; simple resistor networks; Mains electricity and electrical safety
- Light: Electromagnetic spectrum; Reflection; Refraction; Diffraction; Interference
- Measurement: Introduction to the general principles of measurement
