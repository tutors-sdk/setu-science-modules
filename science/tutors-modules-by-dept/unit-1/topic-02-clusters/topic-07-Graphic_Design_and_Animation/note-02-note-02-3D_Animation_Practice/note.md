# 3D Animation Practice

The purpose of this module is to give a student the practical knowledge to prepare 3D Model and audio digital assets for use in rendered 3D animated scenes and to be able to appreciate the theory and practice of 3D animation techniques.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Graphic Design and Animation

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Design, manage and produce short key-framed 3D Animations.
- Apply the key principles of animation to 3D assets and scene.
- Prepare a computer generated character for animation.
- Integrate audio assets within a 3D animated scene.
- Demonstrate the rendering process needed to create a sequence of images.

---

## Indicative Content

- Rigging and Skinning 3D assets
- Real-time polygon mesh textured rendering
- Inverse Kinematics and motion analysis
- Principles of Animation
- Audio: adding soundtracks to scenes
