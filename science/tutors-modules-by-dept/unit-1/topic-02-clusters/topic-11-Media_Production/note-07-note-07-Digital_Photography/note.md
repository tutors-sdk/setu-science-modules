# Digital Photography

The aim of the module is to advance the student?s knowledge and skill in practical digital photography.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Media Production

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Manage the technical aspects of image capture.
- Integrate key visual principals underlying digital image capture and editing.
- Compose images through editing.
- Assess correct camera controls and settings for a subject.
- Develop and realise photographic projects.

---

## Indicative Content

- Camera Controls and operations
- Composition principles
- Practical image making
- Practical editing techniques
- Lighting Techniques -- studio and available light
- Presentation of images
