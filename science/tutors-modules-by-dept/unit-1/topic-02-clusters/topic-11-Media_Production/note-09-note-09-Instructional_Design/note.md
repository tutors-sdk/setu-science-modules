# Instructional Design

Instructional Design is the analysis of how people learn and the systematic development of instruction.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Media Production

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Evaluate key assumptions within the main learning paradigms.
- Assess the practical implications of pedagogical approaches to the effective design of training and learning solutions.
- Evaluate current instructional technologies and assess the practical implications in designing effective training and learning solutions.
- Design and develop an effective training solution based upon theoretical assumptions explored in the module.

---

## Indicative Content

- Theories of learning
- Implications of pedagogical approaches to the effective design of training and learning solutions
- The instructional design process
- Evaluation of current instructional design technologies and delivery systems
