# Video Production

This module provides the student with the opportunity to apply his/her knowledge of the production pipeline.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Introductory
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Media Production

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Demonstrate an understanding of video principles.
- Apply sympathetic audio tracks to video.
- Demonstrate extensive use of industry standard video editing software.
- Apply animation of objects and text utilizing effects, and pre-sets using post production software.
- Construct basic post production cinematic effects for the purpose of branding.
- Complete a short trailer, advert or instructional video for delivery on web, mobile or social media.

---

## Indicative Content

- Shoot and capture video assets
- Video editing: Transitions;Effects;Edits;Motions;Titles;Animations
- Audio: acquire; Edit;Mix;Effects
- Post Production: Colour;Presets;Animation of objects and text
- Toolset for the distortion of objects in post production
- Build and manipulate 3d objects using advanced editing techniques in post production
