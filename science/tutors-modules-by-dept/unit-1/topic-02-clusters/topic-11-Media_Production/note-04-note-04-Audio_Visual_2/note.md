# Audio Visual 2

This module focuses on visual media.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Media Production

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Compare and contrast artwork using design principles.
- Demonstrate practical use of image composition.
- Produce digital video based on a prescribed script/storyboard.
- Combine audio and visual media to create a multi-layered asset.

---

## Indicative Content

- Visual image principles
- Design Principles
- Camera Settings
- Video recording, editing and rendering
- Audio synchronisation
- Titles, transitions and simple motion graphics

---

## Resources Required

- COMPUTER LAB: Multimedia Lab
- EQUIPMENT: MAC PCs
