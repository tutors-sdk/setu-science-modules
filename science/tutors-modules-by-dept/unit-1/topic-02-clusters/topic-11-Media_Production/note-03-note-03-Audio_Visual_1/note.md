# Audio Visual 1

This module teaches the fundamentals of sound, music, sound recording and editing using timeline-based tools and introduces the basics of video production.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Introductory
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Media Production

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Interpret basic sheet music and translate printed sheet music to a digital format.
- Examine the qualities of sound and musical design as they are applied to digital media productions.
- Construct a simple but original musical composition, using designated music hardware and software packages.
- Produce and publish a media asset, which contain sequences of multi-layered audio with some simple visuals.

---

## Indicative Content

- Basic music theory
- Characteristics of sound: amplitude; frequency; timbre
- Sound recording
- Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs)
- Delivery formats
- Basic video production
- Copyright

---

## Resources Required

- EQUIPMENT: MAC PCs
- COMPUTER LAB: Multimedia Lab
