# Business Analytics 2

The purpose is to advance the students skills in conducting business analytics on structured data and to introduce the student to the analysis conducted on unstructured data.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Database and Analytics

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Prepare results from an advanced analytics project using an appropriate Data Mining process.
- Choose and apply appropriate data mining methods to a data set.
- Evaluate results from data mining conducted on a data set.
- Evaluate and determine appropriate advanced data mining techniques.
- Appraise the use of analytics on unstructured data in relation to text and web mining.

---

## Indicative Content

- Theory of advanced data mining methods
- Use of advanced data mining methods in the context of business analytics
- Data mining algorithms: Association Rules; Classification; Clustering;
- Implementation, evaluation and interpretation of results using advanced data mining techniques
- Fundamental of the analysis of unstructured data
- Introduction to Text and Web mining techniques
