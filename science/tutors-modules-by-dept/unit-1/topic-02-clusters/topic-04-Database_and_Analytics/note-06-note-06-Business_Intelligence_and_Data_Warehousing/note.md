# BI & Data Warehousing

This module will introduce Business Intelligence and Data Warehousing concepts and techniques.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 10
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Computing and Mathematics
- **Cluster:** Database and Analytics

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Assess different types of Organisations Memory for BI solutions.
- Evaluate Data Quality Management and ETL utilities for a Data Warehouse.
- Appraise pre-processing and preparation on the data sets.
- Design and develop a Data Warehouse solution in terms of meeting business requirements.
- Appraise and use Data, Text and Web mining techniques and processes.
- Assess different Integration tools and Processing tools for unstructured, semi-structured with structured data.
- Evaluate guidance on Data Governance, Ethics and Policies.

---

## Indicative Content

- Introduction to Business Intelligence (BI) concepts, tools and technologies
- Different types of Organisation memory
- Data Warehouse Architecture: Design and Development
- Data Quality and Management. Process of Extraction, Transformation and Loading (ETL) of a Data Warehouse
- Data Warehouse Implementation, Maintenance and Trends
- Business Intelligence Tools & Techniques
- Data mining -- fundamentals of analytics -- reading datasets, applying techniques required for decision making
- Big data, Text and Web mining -- techniques and data load processes for building analysis.
