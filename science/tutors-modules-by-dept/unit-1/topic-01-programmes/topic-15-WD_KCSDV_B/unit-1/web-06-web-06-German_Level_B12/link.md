German Level B1.2

The module aims to: enable the student to understand the main points of clear standard input on familiar matters regularly encountered in work, school, leisure etc; give the students the skills to deal with most situations likely to arise whilst travelling in an area where the language is spoken; teach the student to produce simple connected text on topics which are familiar or of personal interest; enable to student to describe experiences, feelings and reactions and briefly give reasons for opinions; give the student an understanding of some of the underlying meaning of the other language and images used in film, literature, the media and advertising from the other culture.
