# Semiconductor Fundamentals

The course aims to introduce the student to the fundamentals of solid state theory in support of the analysis and design of semiconductor materials and devices.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Engineering Technology
- **Cluster:** Electronics

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Illustrate the fundamental concepts of solid state theory as applied in the analysis of carrier transport in semiconductor materials.
- Explain the fundamental physical processes employed in the fabrication of semiconductor diode structures.
- Demonstrate sound knowledge of the fundamentals of design, operation and fabrication of schottky, junction and optoelectronic diode devices.
- Apply the knowledge and comprehension gained in evaluating and relating macroscopic semiconductor diode performance parameters to semiconductor solid state theory and associated fabrication processes.
- Utilise the tools and methods employed in carrier transport analysis and process simulation for semiconductor diode structures.

---

## Indicative Content

- Fundamental Semiconductor Theory
- The Schottky Diode
- The Junction Diode
- Optoelectronic Devices
- Semiconductor TCAD Tools
