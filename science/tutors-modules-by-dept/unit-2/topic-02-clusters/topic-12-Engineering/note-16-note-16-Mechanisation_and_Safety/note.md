# Mechanisation and Safety

The module will enable the student to acquire knowledge and awareness to work safely.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Introductory
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Engineering

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Describe safe working methods and practices in accordance with associated legislation.
- Complete manual handling, a risk assessment and Health and Safety statement for a workplace situation.
- Explain working principles and maintenance of power units in agriculture / forestry.
- Describe working principles of hydraulics / pneumatics.
- Explain basic electrics for farm use.
- Describe the operation/ calibration of a pesticide and fertiliser applicator.
- Demonstrate an understanding of safe operation of tractors and machinery.

---

## Indicative Content

- Health & safety
- Risk assessment and manual handling
- Power Units
- Hydraulics and pneumatics
- Electrics
- Pesticide & fertiliser application
- Safe tractor operation
