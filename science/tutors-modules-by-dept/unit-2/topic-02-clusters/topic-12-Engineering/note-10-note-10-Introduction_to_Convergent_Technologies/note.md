# Int to Convergent Technologies

This module aims to build on the students undergraduate knowledge and introduce him / her to the state-of-the-art in convergent technologies for advanced bio-medical and electro-mechanical applications.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Engineering Technology
- **Cluster:** Engineering

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Demonstrate a knowledge and comprehension of the fundamental importance of convergent technologies in the development of advanced bio-medical and electro-mechanical applications.
- Demonstrate a knowledge and comprehension of basic bio-mechanical measurement variables, signal monitoring / waveform generation and data handling technologies that are commonly employed in advanced bio-medical and electro-mechanical applications.
- Demonstrate an ability to apply the knowledge and comprehension gained in analysing advanced bio-medical and electro-mechanical applications and able to relate system specifications to implementation technologies.
- Demonstrate ability to develop system and block level design criteria and partial solutions for selected bio-mechanical applications.
- Demonstrate an understanding of associated regulatory and ethical considerations.

---

## Indicative Content

- Overview of the concept of convergent technologies.
- Review of typical industry driven technology convergence roadmaps.
- Review of key regulatory frameworks and application driven ethical considerations.
- Review of typical bio-mechanical measurement variables.
- System and block level review of available technologies for data handling, signal monitoring and waveform generation in advanced bio-medical and electro-mechanical applications
- Advanced Case Studies (Ex.: structural health monitoring, pulse oximeter, ECG, pacemaker etc.).
