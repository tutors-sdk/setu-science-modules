# Mechanisation

This module will enable the student to acquire a comprehensive knowledge of tractor technology and a detailed understanding of the selection, operation and maintenance of crop establishment, organic manure handling, harvesting and forage production and feeding equipment.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Introductory
- **Department:** Land Sciences
- **Cluster:** Engineering

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Describe modern tractors systems including their component design.
- Explain electronic and precision control systems on modern machinery.
- Describe crop establishment equipment.
- Discuss organic manure handling equipment.
- Describe harvesting equipment and systems.
- Demonstrate an understanding of the machines involved in forage production and feeding.
- Identify workshop hand tools and power tools.

---

## Indicative Content

- Tractor technologies
- Electronics and precision control systems
- Crop establishment equipment
- Organic manure handling equipment
- Harvesting equipment
- Forage production and feeding equipment
- Workshop tools
