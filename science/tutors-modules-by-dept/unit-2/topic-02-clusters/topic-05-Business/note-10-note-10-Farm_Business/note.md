# Farm Business

This module will enable the student to acquire an in-depth knowledge of farm business planning, as well as succession and inheritance issues.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Business

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Interpret final accounts for a farm business.
- Use financial ratios to monitor and control the financial strengths of the farm business.
- Complete a farm business plan.
- Complete a farm succession plan.

---

## Indicative Content

- Farm accounts analysis and evaluation
- Farm business planning
- Farmer succession and inheritance plans
