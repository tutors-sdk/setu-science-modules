# Food Business

This course aims to introduce the student to the strategy of food business in Ireland, the EU and internationally.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Business

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Analyse the strategies of key players operating in the food business, both locally and globally.
- Examine and identify the market forces influencing the development of food business strategy.
- Complete both qualitative and quantitative food business research.
- Develop a food business report and recommend appropriate strategies.
- Prepare presentation and defend report findings.

---

## Indicative Content

- Food business strategy in Ireland, the EU and internationally
- The market forces influencing the main players in both local and global markets
- Research of food market trends and selected food companies' strategies
- Application of both qualitative and quantitative consumer food research methods
- Research, compilation and presentation of a food business report
