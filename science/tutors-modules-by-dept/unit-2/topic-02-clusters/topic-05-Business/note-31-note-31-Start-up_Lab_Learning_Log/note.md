# Start-up Learning Log

The Start-up Lab learning log is an important tool in the development of reflective learning skills.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 10
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Department:** Management and Organisation
- **Cluster:** Business

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Complete a reflective learning log for the flexible semester.
- Appraise own capabilities, competences and skills to start-up and run an enterprise.
- Communicate how they have applied academic and practical knowledge and skills to their own start-up idea.
- Communicate practical learning experiences from undertaking the Start-up Lab module.
- Generate key personal and professional outcomes from undertaking the Start-up Lab module.

---

## Indicative Content

- Overview of the nature and purpose of a reflective learing log
- Overview of key techniques in developing a reflective learning log
- Choice and description of learning activities
- Abstraction of learning to theory and personal development or transferable skills
- Learning logs as a means of personal evaluation and action
