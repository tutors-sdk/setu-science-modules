# Communication Networks (NUIST)

This module concentrates on the Design, Management and Security aspects of Computer Networks.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Postgraduate
- **Department:** Engineering Technology
- **Cluster:** Discipline Specific Technologies

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Survey the salient features of a data communication network, including equipment, tools, standards and protocols.
- Specify and design a small to medium size LAN.
- Design he logic and physical LAN set up in a residential or co-operative environment.
- Assess performance, security and scalability of LAN.
- Optimise the LAN management and configuration.
- Validate the designed LAN through structured professional documentation and oral presentation.

---

## Indicative Content

- Addressing Subnetting, DHCP, NAT, IPv6
- Routing Routing Protocols and Algorithms, Routing Tables
- TCP/IP versions and performance analysis
- Security Protocols IPSEC, TLS, PGP, Firewalls, VPN
- Network Management
- Structure equipment and cabling (Gigabit Ethernet, Wireless Lans IEEE802.11n/g, Bluetooth)
- Software-defined networks
- Congestion Control & QoS
- Project-oriented and top-down network design
- Selected topics on networking at the edge (Internet of Things, Industrial Networks, Body Access Networks)
