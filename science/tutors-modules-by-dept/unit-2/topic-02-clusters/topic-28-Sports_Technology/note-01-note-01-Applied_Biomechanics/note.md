# Applied Biomechanics

The aim of this module is to guide students in assessing and critiquing the applications of biomechanics in research and practice.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Sport and Exercise Science
- **Cluster:** Sports Technology

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Appraise the applications of lab-based biomechanics systems in research and practise.
- Appraise the applications of field-based biomechanics systems in research and practise.
- Prescribe appropriate flexibility/screening assessments for specific population groups.
- Devise an appropriate biomechanical testing protocol to address a specific practical/research issue.
- Explain the findings from a biomechanical testing protocol.

---

## Indicative Content

- Applications of motion analysis in research and practice
- Applications of force platforms in research and practice
- Applications of EMG in research and practice
- Applications of sensor data (e.g. accelerometers, gyroscopes, GPS)
- Applications of portable data collection (e.g. via phones, smart fabrics etc.)
- Gait analysis (Running and Walking)
- Simulation - forward and inverse dynamics
- Applications of flexibility assessment and movement screening
