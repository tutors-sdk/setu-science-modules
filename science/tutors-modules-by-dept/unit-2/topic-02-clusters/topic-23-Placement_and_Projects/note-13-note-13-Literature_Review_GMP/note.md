# Literature Review (GMP)

The aim of this module is for a student to source, review, summarise and discuss peer-reviewed literature on a chosen academic topic.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 10
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Placement and Projects

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Propose a review topic.
- Utilise the library databases to gather, read and analyse literature on the chosen topic.
- Compile the review report, including a bibliography.
- Communicate the review of the literature to academic staff and peers.

---

## Indicative Content

- Literature search; participation in library database tutorials
- Technical and scientific writing
- Scientific referencing
