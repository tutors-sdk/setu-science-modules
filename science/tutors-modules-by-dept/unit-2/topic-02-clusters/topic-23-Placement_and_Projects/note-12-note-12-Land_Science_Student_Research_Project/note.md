# Land Science Research Project

This module consists of a research and development project, which aims to give students the facility to plan and carry out a programme of work based significantly on their own initiative.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 10
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Placement and Projects

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Design and conduct a detailed field-based, laboratory-based, or desktop-based research project.
- Produce a comprehensive and referenced scientific report.
- Prepare a set of coherent results and analyse the data.
- Produce a presentation justifying a thorough understanding of their research methodologies.

---

## Indicative Content

- Experimental design
- Project work
- Written report on project
- Interpretation and critical evaluation of results in the context of the relevant scientific literature
- Presentation of report at seminar and oral
