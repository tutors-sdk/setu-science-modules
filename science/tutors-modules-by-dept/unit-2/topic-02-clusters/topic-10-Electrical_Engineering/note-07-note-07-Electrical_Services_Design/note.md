## Module Aim

The aim of this module is to provide the knowledge and skills necessary to design and layout the electrical services and systems for a modern building/ plant.

---

## Learning Outcomes

On successful completion of this module, learners will be able to:

1. Describe the electrical systems that are found in modern buildings & plant.
2. Apply standard calculation techniques necessary for the design of electrical systems within a modern building.
3. Recognise and understand the comparative cost and operational benefits of different types of electrical services and systems within modern buildings.
4. Explain the procedures involved in designing the electrical services for a building/ plant.
5. Apply electrical services design techniques.

---

## Indicative Content

The module covers the following topics:

- Cables & cable sizing, switchboard & switchgear design
- Electrical Containment Systems
- Fire Alarm Systems
- Lightning protection & UPS systems
- Testing & Certification

---

## Learning and Teaching Methods

Lectures, presentations, reading and problem solving in class.

Site visits and (on site) presentation of specialist equipment and topics

Practical design Exercises

Case study based on the design and layout of electrical services and systems
