# Nutrition

This module introduces the study of nutrition and the foundations of healthy eating.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Introductory
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Food Science

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Define nutrition and discuss the basics of a healthy diet.
- Identify ways of assessing nutritional status.
- Discuss the influences on eating habits.
- Discuss nutrients in various food groups.
- Explain the role of nutrients in health.
- Review diets using dietary analysis programmes.
- Discuss the application of nutritional knowledge across the life span.

---

## Indicative Content

- Study of nutrition and food habits
- Assessing nutritional status
- Basics of a healthy diet
- Dietary analysis, assessing dietary intake
- The nutrients in food and their role in health: proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, minerals
- The application of nutritional knowledge, pregnancy and lactation, infants, children and adolescents, adults and the elderly population
