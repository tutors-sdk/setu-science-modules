# Industrial Automation

The aim of this module is to introduce the student to network topologies, bus types and communication protocols.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Department:** Engineering Technology
- **Cluster:** Automotive, Automation and IoT

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Apply, interface and develop computer network topologies, bus types and communication protocols.
- Integrate with Scada topologies, PLC, electrical distribution, robotic and factory automation examples.
- Integrate with Distributed Control Systems topologies, electrical distribution, robotic and factory automation examples.
- Demonstrate the ability to produce a practical automation system.
- Demonstrate the professional, ethical and effective communication skills of a professional engineer.

---

## Indicative Content

- Review of the control of actuators and sensors using PLCs and Labview.
- Scada Theory Overview
- Introduction seminar on Scada topologies, PLC, electrical distribution, robotic and factory automation examples.
- Develop Scada topologies, PLC, electrical distribution, robotic and factory automation examples.
- Distributed Control systems Theory Overview
- Introduction seminar on Distributed Control Systems topologies, electrical distribution, robotic, vision and factory automation examples.
- Develop Distributed Control Systems topologies, electrical distribution, robotic and factory automation examples.
