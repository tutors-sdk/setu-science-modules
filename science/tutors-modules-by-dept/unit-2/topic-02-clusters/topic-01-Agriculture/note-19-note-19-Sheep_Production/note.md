# Sheep Production

This module will enable the student to acquire a detailed knowledge of production and management practices, and the everyday skills needed to manage a sheep flock.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Introductory
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Agriculture

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Identify feeds and feeding systems for sheep.
- Compare systems of sheep production.
- Employ disease control strategies.
- Examine sheep facilities.
- Complete safely tasks associated with sheep production with due regard to good environmental practice and the animal's health and welfare.
- Calculate the cost of sheep production.
- Describe good environmental practices in sheep production.
- Describe appropriate actions to promote and maintain the good health and welfare of sheep.

---

## Indicative Content

- Feeds and feeding
- Breeding
- Systems of lamb production
- Diseases
- Sheep facilities
- Sheep production tasks
- Economics of sheep production
