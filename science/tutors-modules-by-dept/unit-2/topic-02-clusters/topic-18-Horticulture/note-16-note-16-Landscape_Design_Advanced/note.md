# Landscape Design Advanced

This is a studio based module where students build on knowledge and skills acquired in the 'Landscape Design' major elective.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Horticulture

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Survey and analyse public and semi-public sites.
- Design an appropriate landscape design meeting the needs of the client and working within the constraints of the site.
- Use a variety of drawing types.
- Propose suitable vegetation for a range of landscape contexts.
- Compile a management plan and costings for a selected planting scheme.

---

## Indicative Content

- Draft site surveys and site analysis
- Draft masterplans including section/elevation drawings
- Draft axonometric projections and construction details
- Draft planting plans
- Prepare a maintenance plan for proposed planting scheme
