# Environmental Education

This module will provide students with the necessary skills to teach people about the natural environment using interactive, informal learning activities and materials.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Horticulture

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Propose a range of activities to exploit and explain the natural environment.
- Select a range of materials suitable for use on a site for educational purposes.
- Evaluate an existing interpretative program.
- Propose an educational/interpretative program with associated evaluation methods, for a given group.

---

## Indicative Content

- Identifying good guiding techniques, identifying audiences, tailoring delivery and content to groups and individuals
- Safety issues, hazard identification of any activity
- Interpretation methods and techniques
- Planning activities to meet targets, evaluating activities
