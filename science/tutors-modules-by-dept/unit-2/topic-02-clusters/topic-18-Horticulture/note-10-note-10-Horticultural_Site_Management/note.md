# Horticultural Site Management

A range of Horticultural management problems will be tackled to develop problem solving skills and powers of appraisal.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Horticulture

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Evaluate the security and staff safety issues for a given site.
- Develop an standard operating protocol for a given activity or task.
- Evaluate current work practices in an operation and recommend potential improvements in a professionally produced report.
- Produce a professional report as part of a team effort.

---

## Indicative Content

- Site security and safety
- Work measurement and time and motion study methods, lean theory
- Safe practices, risk assessment
- Standard operating protocols
- Bord Bia - quality programmes
