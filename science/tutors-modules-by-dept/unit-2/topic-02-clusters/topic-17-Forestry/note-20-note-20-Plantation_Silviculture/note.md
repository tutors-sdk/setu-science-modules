# Plantation Silviculture

This module will introduce the whole concept of plantation forestry and the rationales behind it.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Forestry

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Interpret the concepts and reasons for plantation forests.
- Debate the constraints on silviculture due to economic, ecological, and social factors.
- Compare the layout and design of plantation forests.
- Analyse the different types of silvicultural system.
- Contrast specialised forms of plantations.
- Analyse the management of a forest plantation with respect to its composition structure, biodiversity and protection.
- Analyse relevant papers in the field of forest protection.

---

## Indicative Content

- Introduction: the role of plantations
- Production and long-term productivity in plantations
- Rationales for plantation forestry: economic, biodiversity, and social and policy issues
- Layout and design of plantation forests
- Specialised forms of plantation: short rotation crops, plantations on disturbed land
- Silvicultural systems
