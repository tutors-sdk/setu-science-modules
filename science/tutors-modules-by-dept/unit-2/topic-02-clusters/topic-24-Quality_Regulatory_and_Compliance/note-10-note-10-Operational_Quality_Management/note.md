# Operational QM

The aim of this module is to provide the professional adult learner with knowledge relating to quality and operations management, lean manufacturing, six sigma, leadership, strategy and competitive advantage which they can apply to their workplace environment.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 10
- **Level:** Advanced
- **Department:** Science
- **Cluster:** Quality, Regulatory and Compliance

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Appraise the application of Lean Six Sigma and associated systems in the workplace.
- Critique the impact of strategic management and effective leadership.
- Evaluate adaptive organisational culture.
- Assess change management initiatives using case studies.
- Interpret the application of performance metrics to maximise competitive advantage.

---

## Indicative Content

- Lean Management Systems, Six Sigma, Operational Excellence
- Strategy and Leadership
- Organisational culture and management
- Change Management, global context for change
- Performance Management
