# Developmental Psychology

This module aims to: Introduce students to the main theories, methods and concepts used to understand human development.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Introductory
- **Department:** Arts
- **Cluster:** Humanities

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Identify each stage of development in the context of the lifespan of the individual.
- Summarise conceptual frameworks, methodologies and research within developmental psychology.
- Differentiate applications of developmental theories in applied settings.
- Discuss conceptual frameworks of lifespan developmental theory and their application in applied settings.

---

## Indicative Content

- Introduction to theories of Human Development
- Prenatal Development
- Physical Development in Infancy and Early Childhood
- Cognitive Development in Childhood
- Development of Language, Self and Social Cognition
- Attachment
- Development of Gender
- Disorders of Childhood
- Adolescence
- Adulthood
- Old Age
- Conclusion/Recap
