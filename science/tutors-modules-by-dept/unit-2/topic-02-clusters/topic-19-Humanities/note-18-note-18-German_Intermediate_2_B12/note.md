# German B1.2

This module aims to enable students to speak, write, listen and read German to level B1.2 of the Common European Framework Reference (CEFR) for Languages.

---

## Module Details

- **Credits:** 5
- **Level:** Introductory
- **Department:** Law and Criminal Justice Studies
- **Cluster:** Humanities

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Speaking: Develop an argument, engage in discussion and explain choices on non-routine topics in conversations and presentations.
- Writing: Write texts and summarise shorter descriptive and/or argumentative articles and express personal opinion.
- Listening: Follow the main points in authentic media clips.
- Reading: Extract and respond to authentic material in German.
- Demonstrate intercultural awareness and independent reflection.

---

## Indicative Content

- Themes that will be used to achieve the learning outcomes for this module will include:
- Job applications
- Geography and Place
- Climate and environment
- Aspects of German speaking cultures including the German Education System
- Linguistic structures will build on those introduced in Level B1.1. Additional forms to be covered in this module include:
- Conjunctions including two-part conjunctions
- adverbial agreements
- Passive with modal verbs
- Genitive case
